# International Council for Research Innovation in Sci, tech and Engg (ICRI-STE) help disk online

A structured support mechanism implemented through Google Docs, incorporating commenting tools, response options, and standardized guidelines, 
supported by collaboration and communication via Microsoft Teams and Zoom. 
Regular meetings are scheduled on Saturdays and/or Sundays, with flexible booking for participants.
